Our Mission

Myrmex is a revolutionary Research Colony dedicated to exploring natural solutions to human problems. Within our Research Hives, we work to unlock the secrets to the remarkable abilities of the Insect Kingdom. Utilizing advances in Genetics and Bioengineering, we hope that to create products that will better humanity; leaving our species able to tackle future challenges.
